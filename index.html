About me

Hi there! I’m a frontend developer and UI/UX designer with a knack for turning creative ideas into functional, user-friendly digital experiences. My journey into the tech world began with a passion for understanding how things work under the hood, and that curiosity led me to fall in love with web development and design.

I specialize in crafting intuitive and visually appealing interfaces, and I’ve had the pleasure of working on projects like the Naijanest travel app and the Akwacross food app. Whether I’m designing an engaging app or building responsive websites, I always aim to create solutions that make life easier for users.

Beyond coding and design, I’m constantly learning about the latest in AI technology, especially in areas like AI-powered brain health solutions, which fascinates me. In fact, I’ve been working on a project called Brain with AI, where I blend tech and health to offer intelligent solutions for improving cognitive wellness.

When I’m not deep in code, I’m usually organizing fun events like picnics to celebrate hardworking girls or diving into the latest trends in tech and design.
